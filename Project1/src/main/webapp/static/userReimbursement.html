Reimbursements

Reimbursements with a status of 1, 2, and 3 are either approved, denied, or pending respectively. When adding a reimbursement, valid TypeIds are 1, 2, 3, or 4 for food, general, travel, or lodging respectively.

Managers, please enter the ID of the reimbursement you wish to resolve as well as the new status ID. Entering 1, 2, or 3 as the status ID will set the reimbursement to approved, denied, or pending respectively. Managers cannot resolve their own reimbursements.
